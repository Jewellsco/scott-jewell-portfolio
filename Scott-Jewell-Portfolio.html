About

Designer · Artist · Father
I'm a designer with over a decade in visual design and a decade in UI. I thrive on simplifying complex apps, and I care about the customer journey and the details that make products feel effortless. Creative, collaborative, and dedicated to outstanding user experiences.

From print and visual design into product, I've spent fifteen years learning what makes interfaces feel inevitable: research, clear hierarchy, accessible patterns, and a relentless focus on the person on the other side of the screen.

Today I design for healthcare and fintech, lead 0 → 1 product work, and bring AI-first practices into how I design and ship.
