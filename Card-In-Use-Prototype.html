Mobile App Redesign

Redesigned the account, profile, and sign in experience used by millions, with an animated, interactive prototype that recreates the flow screen by screen.

Role
Senior Product Designer
Platform
iOS and Android
Focus
Accounts, identity, security
Scale
Millions of users
Overview

Make a daily, high stakes flow feel effortless

Account, profile, and sign in are the doors to the whole CVS app, and they carry real security weight. I redesigned these flows for iOS and Android so they feel calm and quick, then built an animated prototype that recreates the experience screen by screen so the team could feel it, not just review static frames.

Biometric and secure sign in

Faster entry that still meets a high security bar, designed for both platforms.

Profile and account clarity

A cleaner information hierarchy so people find and change the right thing without second guessing.

Code backed prototype

A real, tappable prototype that tested the flow before engineering invested.

Accessible by default

Contrast, targets, and labels checked so the experience works for everyone.
