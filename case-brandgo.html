Overview

One place to find, buy, and build a wardrobe

While leading work on Freebird's site, mobile app, design system, and rewards program, I spotted another need: shoppers were juggling dozens of apps to assemble a look. Brand GO brings the entire catalogue of online apparel into one place — a single home to discover, buy, and build your ultimate wardrobe.

"What if finding the perfect piece felt less like ten browser tabs and more like one social feed?"
Brand GO — concept
Challenge & vision

From scattered to single-source

Apparel discovery was fragmented across brands and apps. The vision for Brand GO was a unified, social-first experience: a Discover page that surfaces the right pieces, social shopping that lets people shop together, and peer reviews that build trust before you buy.

The experience

Discover, shop, and review — together

The designs bring discovery and community into the same flow: a feed-like Discover experience, social shopping that turns buying into something you do with friends, and user reviews that make every product decision more confident.

Reflection

What I took from it

Brand GO came from noticing a problem nobody had scoped yet — the best product ideas often start as an itch you feel as a user. Designing a social-commerce concept stretched me beyond execution into product vision: defining what to build and why, not just how it looks.
