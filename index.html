Notes from the designer
Why this project is different
The work

Founder work means no brief and no handoff. I designed the onboarding, the money movement, and the brand, then shipped them as one person with an AI stack instead of a team.

The challenge

A money product has to earn a family's trust before it asks them to move a single dollar. Every flow is written in plain language and shows where the money sits at each step.

The design system

One green on black system runs the site, the app, and the card. Tokens first, so a solo designer can stay consistent at speed.

About

Designer · Artist · Father
I'm a designer with over a decade in visual design. I thrive on simplifying complex apps, and I care about the customer journey and the details that make products feel effortless. Creative, collaborative, and dedicated to outstanding user experiences.

From print and visual design into product, I've spent fifteen years learning what makes interfaces feel inevitable: research, clear hierarchy, accessible patterns, and a relentless focus on the person on the other side of the screen.

Today I design for healthcare and fintech, lead 0 → 1 product work, and use AI to ship faster without lowering the bar on trust or craft.
