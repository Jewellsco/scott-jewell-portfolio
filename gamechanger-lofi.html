I keep this stage deliberately ugly. Its only job is picking the right problem and the right flows before a single pixel gets styled. React to the thinking, not the boxes.

01 · The opportunitywhere my teardown pointed
Working title, Worth every dollar

Families pay about 99 dollars a year, resent the price, and generate nine million games of data doing it. I am designing the insight system that makes the family feel the subscription pay for itself every week, through the coach, the player, and the household.

I did not invent the problem. Price resentment is in their reviews. Demand for deeper stats is in their reviews. The insight surfaces are already shipping. The multi kid clutter complaint is in their reviews. Every claim traces to something public.
